About Freedomfighter

They were known as the Mukti Bahini (Liberation Army), a resistance force made up of soldiers, students, farmers, and ordinary citizens who took up arms to defend their homeland. Despite limited resources, their courage and determination turned the tide of history, leading to the birth of Bangladesh as a sovereign nation. Their sacrifices—many giving their lives—remain a source of pride and inspiration, symbolizing resilience, patriotism, and the unyielding spirit of the Bengali people.
