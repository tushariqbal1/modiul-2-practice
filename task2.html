Bangladesh is a beautiful South Asian country known for its rich culture, resilient history, and natural wonders. With Dhaka as its bustling capital, it is home to more than 170 million people who speak Bengali and celebrate traditions rooted in art, music, and literature. The nation is famous for the Sundarbans mangrove forest, the Royal Bengal Tiger, and its countless rivers that shape fertile lands. Despite challenges like floods and cyclones, Bangladesh has shown remarkable progress in education, health, and economic growth, becoming one of the world’s fastest‑developing countries.
